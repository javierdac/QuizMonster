Ten warning signs of a Teen abusive relationship

  1. History of legal or discipline problems.
  2. Blames you for his/her anger.
  3. Serious drug or alcohol abuse.
  4. History of violent behavior.
  5. Threatens others regularly.
  6. Insults you or calls you names.
  7. Trouble controlling feeling like anger.
  8. Tells you what to wear, what to do or how to act.
  9. Threatens or intimidates you in order to get their way.
  10. Prevents you from spending time with your friends or family.

Create a safely plan

  1. Talk to someone that you can trust.
  2. Plan in advance to have a safe place to go.
  3. Keep money and your cellphone or calling card with you all times.
  4. Memorize important numbers in case your phone is taken.
  5. Establish a code word or sign so family, friends an co-workers know when to call for help.
